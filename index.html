PACKING HACKS


Roll, Don't Fold !
- Maximize suitcase space by rolling your clothes, minimizing wrinkles, and creating a more organized pack.

Ziplock for Organization
- Use ziplock bags to compartmentalize items like toiletries,accessories, and electronics for easy access and efficient packing.

Layering is the key!
- Wear your bulkier items during travel to save space in your luggage and stay prepared for fluctuating temperatures.

Travel-sized Essentials
- Invest in travel-sized toiletries and essentials to save space and comply with airline regulations, ensuring a hassle-free journey.
